Accessibility

Everyone should be able to learn here.

We are working toward a clear, keyboard-friendly, readable experience on the website and in the learning content.

Our target

We use WCAG 2.2 Level AA as a design target for the website. This is a goal and not a claim that every page currently conforms. The app and website are different products and may have different platform limitations.

What we do today

  • Use semantic headings, descriptive page titles, responsive layouts, and visible keyboard focus.
  • Respect reduced-motion preferences and keep the worksheet printable without navigation or consent overlays.
  • Keep text contrast, tap targets, and line lengths in mind on small screens.
  • Provide plain-language alternatives to product and AI terminology.

Known limitations

Some older landing pages still contain repeated legacy markup and may need additional screen-reader and navigation testing. App accessibility can vary by operating system, device, and game component. We are tracking improvements in the changelog.

Feedback and assistance

Tell us what blocked you, including the page, device/browser, assistive technology (if relevant), and an accessible way to reply. Email info@vizancia.ca. We will do our best to provide the information in another format while we investigate.
