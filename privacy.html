privacy policy

How we handle information when you use the Vizancia mobile application.

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Vizancia ("Vizancia", "we", "our", or "us") builds an educational mobile application that teaches artificial intelligence. We designed it to work without accounts, tracking, or data collection — and we think a privacy policy should be just as straightforward. This policy explains, in plain language, what information the Vizancia app ("the App") does and does not handle, and how we will tell you if that ever changes.

The short version

  • We do not collect personal information about you.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.
  • Your learning progress stays on your device.
  • We will never quietly start collecting your data. If our practices change, we will update this policy and say so clearly before the change takes effect.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit personal information about you. As of the date above, the App does not:

  • Collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, or location
  • Use analytics, advertising, tracking, or profiling tools
  • Require an account, login, or sign-up
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Send your activity to servers we operate (we do not run servers that receive your data)

If we ever introduce a feature that requires collecting personal information, we will describe what we collect and why in this policy, and we will request any consent required by law before that feature becomes active.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Everything you do in the App — including learning progress, XP, achievements, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. This information never leaves your device through the App, and we cannot access it. Deleting the App removes this data from your device.

3. Apple Game Center

Vizancia optionally integrates with Apple Game Center to power 1v1 duels and global leaderboards. If you choose to use these features, Game Center is operated by Apple and your use of it is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive, store, or transmit your Game Center identity, friends, or scores on any server of our own.

4. Purchases & Premium Features

Vizancia is free to download and includes a free tier of content. We may offer optional premium features (such as additional games or expanded AI Sandbox access) through in-app purchases or subscriptions. If you make a purchase:

  • All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store using your Apple Account. We never see or receive your full payment card details.
  • To deliver and restore purchases, Apple may share limited transaction information with us (for example, confirmation that a purchase was completed or is active). This is used only to unlock the features you bought.
  • To know which premium features to enable, the App may store a record of your entitlements on your device or check your purchase status with Apple. This does not identify you to us personally.

Buying premium features does not change our approach to your privacy: we still do not collect personal information or track you, and we will update this policy before introducing anything that would.

5. Third-Party Services

Vizancia does not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, and the App does not embed third-party trackers. Apart from Apple's own services (such as the App Store and, optionally, Game Center), no third party receives your information through the App. If we ever integrate a third-party service that processes personal information, we will name that service in this policy and explain what it does before we enable it.

6. Children's Privacy

Vizancia is suitable for users of all ages and contains only educational content about artificial intelligence. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the minimum age that applies where you live). Because the App does not collect personal data, no special parental action is required.

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit your personal data, there is no central database of user information for us to lose. Your on-device data is protected by your device's own security features, such as your passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

8. Your Choices

You are always in control. You can use the App fully without an account, choose not to use Game Center, and remove all stored data at any time by deleting the App.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves — for example, when we add optional paid features. When we make a material change, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, highlight the change in the App. We will not retroactively reduce the protections that applied to information we previously promised not to collect.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@vizancia.ca.
