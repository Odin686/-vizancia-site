Product changelog

What changed, in plain language.

Not every code change appears here. This log highlights user-facing product, privacy, accessibility, marketing, and learning-resource updates.

UpdatesJuly 14 · Professional siteJuly 14 · Learning HubMay 31 · Store choice

July 14, 2026 · Professional site and trust update

  • Rebuilt the homepage and homeschool experience around a shared professional design system, sample lessons, curriculum proof, and explicit store choice.
  • Qualified privacy, offline, price, and Canadian-company claims so connected features are not hidden behind an absolute promise.
  • Added the AI Sandbox Safety and Acceptable Use Policy, adult-managed beta boundary, reporting guidance, and expanded AI terms.
  • Added age-aware AI literacy, verification, family agreement, and AI hallucination guides to the Learning Hub.
  • Improved small-text contrast, structured data, social metadata, keyboard fallbacks, and mobile layouts.
  • Added Google Ads conversion-event plumbing. Conversion destinations still require the labels created inside the Google Ads account.

July 14, 2026 · Website trust and Learning Hub

  • Added parent, homeschool, safety, glossary, research, and printable worksheet resources.
  • Added About, Educational Methodology, Editorial Policy, Accessibility, and Product Changelog pages.
  • Added a custom 404 page and reduced-motion safeguards.
  • Clarified the difference between the offline-first core app, optional connected features, and consent-controlled website measurement.

May 31, 2026 · Store choice and consent foundations

  • Made Apple and Google Play links explicit so visitors can choose their store on desktop or mobile.
  • Added consent-controlled Google Ads measurement and a persistent privacy choices control.
  • Removed unverified rating summaries and testimonials from landing-page copy.
