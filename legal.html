Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vizancia free?

Yes — Vizancia is free to download, and all current learning content, mini-games, and duels are included at no cost. We may add optional paid features in the future; if we do, we'll make it clear what's included for free.

Do I need an account?

No. Vizancia works entirely offline with no account or login required. Game Center sign-in is optional if you want to play 1v1 duels or compete on global leaderboards.

What devices are supported?

Vizancia runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 17.0 or later.

Is my data collected?

No. Vizancia does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All progress, including XP, achievements, and streaks, is saved locally on your device.

How do 1v1 duels work?

Sign in with Game Center, then challenge friends or get matched with random opponents. You can also play against AI bots at three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, or Hard.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at info@vizancia.ca with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are always helpful.
