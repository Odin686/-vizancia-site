About Vizancia

AI literacy should build confidence without encouraging blind trust.

Vizancia Incorporated is an Ontario company creating short, practical learning experiences that help people understand how AI works, question its output, protect private information, and keep human responsibility visible.

What we are building

An app and resource library for active AI learning.

The Vizancia app combines lessons, quizzes, mini-games, progress tools, connected challenges, and an optional limited-beta Sandbox. The Learning Hub extends that work with source-backed family guides, homeschool plans, and printable activities.

Educational approach

Teach the judgment, not just the tool.

Our progression moves through understand, practise, reflect, and apply. Every activity should help a learner explain a concept, identify what needs checking, choose a safer action, or make the human contribution clear.

Team and accountability
