A clear boundary

Education and practical readiness—not a compliance certificate.

Vizancia helps people understand AI, identify questions, improve everyday practices, and plan next steps. A program does not certify legal compliance, replace a security assessment, or approve a particular AI system for sensitive use.

Good fit

Foundational literacy, safer use, response checking, prompting, family guidance, and team discussion.

Needs a specialist

Legal opinions, penetration testing, regulated-risk approval, procurement certification, or incident response.
