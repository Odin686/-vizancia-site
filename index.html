Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vizancia free?

Yes! Vizancia is free to download with all learning content included. No ads, no subscriptions.

Do I need an account?

No. Vizancia works entirely offline with no account required. Game Center is optional for duels and leaderboards.

What devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad with iOS 17.0 or later.

Is my data collected?

No. Vizancia does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All progress is saved locally on your device.

How do duels work?

Sign in with Game Center, then challenge friends or get matched with random opponents. You can also play against AI bots at three difficulty levels.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Vizancia ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Vizancia operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Use tracking or analytics tools
  • Require account creation or login
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar technologies
  • Connect to external servers

Data Storage

All app data (learning progress, XP, achievements, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in data storage. This data is never transmitted off your device.

Game Center

Vizancia optionally integrates with Apple Game Center for multiplayer duels and leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple and governed by Apple's privacy policy. Vizancia does not access or store your Game Center data on any external server.

Children's Privacy

Vizancia does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for users of all ages as it contains only educational content about artificial intelligence.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@vizancia.ca.
