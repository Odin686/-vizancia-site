privacy policy

How we handle information when you use the Vizancia mobile application.

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Vizancia ("we", "our", "us", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use the Vizancia mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Vizancia operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Use tracking, analytics, or advertising tools
  • Require account creation or login
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Connect to external servers for data collection

2. Local Data Storage

All app data, including learning progress, XP, achievements, streaks, and settings, is stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in data storage (UserDefaults). This data is never transmitted off your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Apple Game Center

Vizancia optionally integrates with Apple Game Center for multiplayer duels and global leaderboards. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Vizancia does not access, store, or transmit your Game Center data on any external server.

4. Children's Privacy

Vizancia does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The app is suitable for users of all ages as it contains only educational content about artificial intelligence.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breach from our end. Your learning data remains on your device, protected by your device's built-in security measures (passcode, Face ID, etc.).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@vizancia.ca.
