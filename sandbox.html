The learning experience

More than an open chat box.

The Sandbox is being shaped around deliberate practice: give learners room to explore while adding the prompts and pauses that turn AI use into AI literacy.

01

Ask with purpose

Choose a goal, improve a prompt, and see how small changes in context can change the result.

02

Inspect the response

Look for assumptions, missing evidence, and language that sounds more certain than the facts support.

03

Build human judgment

Compare answers, verify important claims, and decide when a person or trusted source should take over.

Designed safeguards

Responsible exploration requires more than a filter.

The beta is designed around layered controls that reduce avoidable risk and reinforce safer habits. These measures help; they cannot make generative AI error-free or appropriate for every situation.

Questions

Before you request access

The Sandbox is an evolving beta. Features, availability, and limits may change as we learn from early users.

Visit Support
Who is the beta for?

Early access is intended for adults and adult-managed family or educator settings that want to explore AI with an emphasis on critical thinking and responsible use. The Sandbox is not authorized for independent use by a child under 13. Availability may be limited during testing.

Is the Sandbox a source of expert advice?

No. It is an educational tool, not a substitute for professional, medical, legal, financial, or other expert advice. Important decisions should be checked with a qualified person.

Does it need an internet connection?

Yes. Unlike Vizancia lessons and most games, the Sandbox must connect to an AI service to process a prompt and return a response.

Can safeguards catch every problem?

No. Layered controls can reduce risk, but no automated system catches everything. Learners should avoid sensitive information and verify consequential claims.

How do I report an unsafe response?

Use the Sandbox report control when it is available, or email info@vizancia.ca. Remove unnecessary personal information before sending a report. See the Sandbox Safety Policy.

How do I request early access?

Email info@vizancia.ca. Tell us what device you use and what you hope to explore. We will reply if a beta place is available.
