About Vizancia

Learning about AI should feel empowering.

Vizancia is built in Canada for families and learners who want a practical, privacy-conscious way to understand artificial intelligence.

What we are building

The Vizancia app combines short lessons, quizzes, games, and an optional sandbox so learners can practise asking questions and checking answers. The AI Learning Hub extends that work with family conversations, homeschool activities, and plain-language explanations.

Who is behind it

Vizancia Incorporated is the publisher of the app and website. We are intentionally not presenting unverified biographies, credentials, testimonials, or ratings. As the team and educational reviewers grow, this page will identify them by name, role, relevant experience, and the work they reviewed.

Our commitments

  • Keep learning data in the app on the learner’s device where the product design allows.
  • Explain limitations and uncertainty instead of promising that AI is always correct.
  • Use age-aware examples and encourage adult involvement for high-stakes topics.
  • Link educational claims to primary research or public guidance.
  • Improve accessibility, clarity, and mobile performance over time.
