Safety First

5 layers of safety filtering.

Every conversation is designed to pass through multiple safety filters; before, during, and after the AI responds.

🛡️

System-Level Rules

The AI will operate under strict safety instructions designed to resist tampering and jailbreak attempts.

🚫

Input Filtering

Every message is scanned before reaching the AI. Blocked categories: violence, explicit content, personal info, and jailbreak attempts.

✅

Output Filtering

AI responses are checked for safety before being shown. Anything flagged is replaced with a safe, friendly message.

⏱️

Rate Limiting

Daily message limits prevent overuse and unexpected costs. Users see a friendly counter showing remaining messages.

🔒

No Data Stored

We don't save your conversations. When the app closes, the chat disappears. Messages are processed by our third-party AI provider to generate a reply and are not stored by us.

👁️

PII Detection

If someone accidentally types a phone number, email, or address, the system is designed to block it and explain why.
