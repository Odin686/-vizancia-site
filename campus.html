Good to know

Before you explore

The Campus screens shown here are from Vizancia 3.5. Details can change as the release is finalized and may differ by platform.

Is the Campus available now?

The Campus is part of Vizancia 3.5. The current Vizancia app is available now, while Campus availability depends on the version offered on your device's app store.

Is the Campus an open chat room?

No. It is a single-player navigation and learning layer. Campus characters are part of the game experience, not other users or an open social chat.

Do Campus Coins use real money?

No. Campus Coins are pretend rewards earned through Campus missions. They are not purchased with real money.

Does Vizancia need an account?

No Vizancia account is required for core lessons. Core progress is stored on the device; connected services are identified separately in the Privacy Policy.
